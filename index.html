Financial Assistance Programs

Eligibility Grant Quiz Please note: UTS College, UTS Exchange (Inbound) and UTS Students on Leave of Absence are not eligible to apply.

Grants

UTS offers financial support programs to assist your short term needs when facing cash flow problems

Vouchers assist with essential items such as groceries and OPAL transport credit.

To apply:

  • Contact reception at 02 9514 1177 to schedule an in-person appointment with Financial Assistance.
  • Eligibility requires demonstrating the most recent 90 days of transactions from all Australian bank accounts in your name.


    Eligibility criteria:
  • Available to international and domestic (local) students currently enrolled.
  • Demonstrated low income (up to AUD$1,200 per fortnight from allowances, work, or financial support) with inadequate savings.
  • Collection of vouchers must occur at UTS Sydney campus.
  • Provide the most recent 90-day bank transaction records at the appointment.

    Note: Students needing additional funds for course-related living expenses may consider applying for a loan.

    • The Financial Support Grant (FSG) provides assistance to eligible local students for essential living costs and education-related expenses such as required texts, placement/internship/project costs, and extraordinary support needs.

    To apply:

  • New students starting their first session should contact Financial Assistance directly. Applications start after the Census Date for continuing students.
  • Contact Tel: 02 9514 1177 before or after application submission.


    Eligibility criteria:
  • Enrolled in an award course with the current teaching session commenced.
  • Currently residing in Australia, exceptions may apply for study-related reasons.
  • Demonstrated low income: Combined income up to AUD$1,200 per fortnight from allowances, work, or financial support, with inadequate savings to cover essential needs.
  • Applicants slightly above the income threshold may be considered based on extenuating circumstances (e.g., caregiving roles).

    • The UTS Housing Rental Subsidy offers financial support to eligible domestic (local) students, New Zealand students, Permanent Residents, and Humanitarian visa holders residing in Yura Mudang.

    To apply:

  • New students starting their first session should contact Financial Assistance directly.
  • Contact Tel: 02 9514 1177 before or after application submission.


    Eligibility criteria:
  • Available for domestic (local) students, New Zealand students, Permanent Residents, and Humanitarian visa holders.
  • Must meet Financial Support Grant (FSG) eligibilities.
  • Requires a current lease with UTS Housing Service and residence in Yura Mudang.
  • Maximum of 2 subsidy awards per year (January to June and July to December sessions).
  • Applicants must demonstrate low income: combined income up to AUD$1,200 per fortnight from allowances, work, or financial support, with inadequate savings to cover essential needs.
  • Applicants slightly above the income threshold may be considered based on extenuating circumstances (e.g., caregiving roles).


    • To apply:

  • For hardware/laptops, appointments are essential. Contact the reception desk on 02 9514 1177 to schedule a telephone appointment or an on-campus meeting with Financial Assistance.


    Eligibility criteria:
  • Available to domestic (local) students, New Zealand onshore students, Permanent Residents, Humanitarian visa holders, and Unistart students.
  • Currently enrolled in a teaching session of an award course.
  • Must demonstrate low income: combined income up to AUD$1,200 per fortnight from allowances, work, or financial support, with inadequate savings for essential needs.
  • Not in the final session of completing a degree or enrolled in a degree of 24 credit points or less.
  • Eligible for hardware/laptops, software, and Internet access costs required for course-related technical needs.
  • Specific hardware, including Windows PC/laptops with standard specifications, or higher if required by the course, or up to $1,000 grant for hardware.
  • Additional support of up to $500 available for software and internet access.
  • Contact reception on 02 9514 1177 for further inquiries or to discuss individual digital requirements and personal circumstances.

    • Application Process

    Explore the application process below.

  • We ask you to tell us about the circumstantial changes that are affecting your financial situation.

  • Upload the most recent 90 days of income and outgoing transactions for each of your Australian bank accounts

  • If you are in receipt of Centrelink you will be required to provide your Customer Reference Number (CRN) and a copy of your most recent Centrelink Income Statement.

  • Provide quotes / prices / receipts for specific requests such as course-related hardware (e.g. sewing machine; camera) under the digital access requests; or material costs for projects under the Financial Support Grant

  • You will need to provide your nominated bank account details in the application (account holder name, BSB and account number) for transfers. This must be an Australian bank account.

  • Applicants for the UTS Housing subsidy will need to supply a copy of their current lease (copy of contract identifying you and the address, the rent cost and the dates of the lease contract)

    • Online applications are available for International Students and Domestic Students

  • We recommend using Chrome, Firefox or Safari (as internet browsers) when completing applications and for the purposes of receiving correspondence from us when processing your application (Internet Explorer is NOT fully compatible with our online application software).

  • You will be able to save and resume your application later. Un-submitted or incomplete applications will accessible for you to re-visit within each 6-month period. That is: On 30 June and 31 December your un-submitted application will be deleted. You will need to start a new application after each of these dates.

  • If further clarification is required to assess your application, you may be asked for further details via the application email or asked to make an appointment with the Financial Assistance Service.
